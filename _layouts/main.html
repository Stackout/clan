10/14/2002, by Xovenia.

Welcome to SP-O's new website. Here you can find information about our clan, skins, maps, mods (downloads), screenshots, and simply whatever is related to our clan. We hope you enjoy.. :)

The history of (SP-O):

Squad Leader used to play Renegade just a bit at first, and once he was getting better at the game he joined a clan, which in his opinion was brilliant. They played together and had strong allies, but slowly more and more of the clan's members started disappearing. Then, slowly the clan started falling apart, but it was fun while it lasted. Some people might remember him, his name was Colonel Gunner, and (in his opinion..) he was kicking ass at Renegade. But this time, he is kicking ass at Renegade, together with his very own kickass squad (our own kickass squad .. :) -Xov.).

He formed the clan SP-O (which stands for Special Operations) somewhere at the end of August, begin September, and it quickly started getting more and more members. (SP-O)'s second (first if you don't count Squad Leader) member was female (QueenAftershock), and she quickly started recruiting a lot of good players for the team, and she is capable of doing some serious shooting herself. Squad Leader was very pleased with this first member, and as SP-O stands now, it has a lot of good players, who might just need some more practice to unlock their true potential.

Squad Leader's story, written by Xovenia.
